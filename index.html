About This Application
Purpose, technology stack, and project information

🎯 Purpose & Mission

AI-Powered Radiology Report Audit & Quality Analyzer is a clinical quality assurance tool designed to help radiologists and QA teams evaluate radiology reports before they are finalized and signed.


It uses Groq (Llama 3.3 70B) or Google Gemini AI to analyze reports across multiple quality dimensions — identifying missing sections, flagging ambiguous terminology, detecting logical mismatches between findings and impressions.


Important: This tool does not generate radiology reports or clinical diagnoses. It is purely an evaluative QA assistant.

📊 Quality Dimensions Evaluated

📋  Completeness — all mandatory sections present
🔬  Medical Terminology — standard clinical language
⚖️  Clinical Alignment — findings ↔ impression consistency
📐  Formatting — structure and template compliance
🎯  Impression Quality — conclusion completeness
